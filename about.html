About Me

Academic Background

Currently, I am pursuing a comprehensive degree in full-stack web development, focusing on mastering both front-end and back-end technologies. My studies emphasize hands-on learning with real-world projects, while also sharpening my skills in UI/UX design, data management, and system security. This blend of technical knowledge and practical experience ensures that I’m ready to tackle complex web development challenges.

Developer Experience

I developed ECORIDE, a robust carpooling platform that combines a secure PHP/JS backend with a modern front-end interface. This project showcases my ability to handle complex features like real-time mapping, role-based access control, credit systems, and notification management. ECORIDE exemplifies my commitment to writing clean, maintainable code that delivers both functionality and an excellent user experience.

E-commerce Project

One of my proudest projects is an e-commerce site that combines a modern, minimalist aesthetic with interactive features. I integrated smooth CSS animations, dynamic product displays, and user-friendly filters to create an engaging shopping experience. From responsive layouts to seamless navigation, this project reflects my dedication to detail and performance optimization.

Designer Touch

My design philosophy centers on clarity, functionality, and user enjoyment. I craft interfaces that are not only visually appealing but also intuitive and efficient. From wireframes to final design, I ensure that every pixel serves a purpose and that the overall experience is seamless across devices. My work merges creativity with usability, resulting in digital spaces where users feel at ease and engaged.
